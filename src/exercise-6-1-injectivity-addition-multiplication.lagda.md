## Problem statement

Show that

```text
  (m = n) ↔ (m + k = n + k)
  (m = n) ↔ (m · (k + 1) = n · (k + 1))
```

for all `m, n, k : ℕ`.
In other words, adding `k` and multiplying by `k + 1` are injective functions.
